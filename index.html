A LITTLE ABOUT ME

I am an Indigenous (Syilx Okanagan and Cree) actress and screenwriter for film, television, theatre and commercial based out of Vancouver BC. I have been into acting for about a decade, my passion starting at a young age of 10 years old. I love being on set and getting into a good emotional scene, my favourite being the gritty and high energetic characters. If I'm not acting, I read books and write, go for walks along the ocean and sometimes play video games. I'm following my dreams in hopes to inspire other young indigenous youth and follow their dreams to their full potential.
